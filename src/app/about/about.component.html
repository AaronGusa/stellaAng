About Stella Vi

Welcome to StellaVi, a small business that is dedicated to supporting and empowering estheticians businesses in local communities. Our mission is to connect estheticians and help them gain exposure through our innovative services and solutions.

Founded in 2022, StellaVi is committed to providing top-notch services that meet the unique needs of estheticians. Our team of experienced professionals works tirelessly to deliver exceptional results and support the growth and success of estheticians businesses.

Our Vision

At StellaVi, our vision is to become the go-to platform for estheticians to connect, collaborate, and succeed. We believe in creating a supportive community where estheticians can learn from each other, share their experiences, and grow their businesses together.

Our Services

  • Business Listing: We provide a comprehensive business listing platform that allows estheticians to showcase their services, location, and contact information to potential clients.
  • Marketing Support: We offer marketing support services, including social media promotion, online advertising, and brand development, to help estheticians increase their visibility and attract more clients.
  • Education and Training: We provide educational resources, training materials, and workshops to help estheticians enhance their skills, stay up-to-date with industry trends, and grow their businesses.
  • Events and Networking: We organize networking events, workshops, and seminars to foster connections among estheticians, provide learning opportunities, and promote collaboration.
